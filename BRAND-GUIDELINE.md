# GrapsFinder

## Brand Guidelines & Design System v1.0

---

# Brand Overview

## Mission

Help wrestling fans discover live wrestling events quickly and easily.

GrapsFinder serves as the central discovery platform for professional wrestling events, from major independent promotions to local shows and community events.

---

## Vision

Become the definitive destination for discovering live wrestling events.

Whether a fan is looking for a show tonight, planning a wrestling weekend, or exploring new promotions, GrapsFinder should be the first place they check.

---

## Core Principles

### Information First, Artwork Second

GrapsFinder exists to help fans discover wrestling events.

Every screen should prioritize:

* Event information
* Searchability
* Clarity
* Speed of discovery

Artwork should enhance information, never replace it.

---

### Mobile First

The primary experience is mobile.

All screens are designed mobile-first and expanded for larger devices.

---

### Independent Wrestling Authenticity

GrapsFinder should feel rooted in wrestling culture.

Inspired by:

* Independent wrestling posters
* Punk rock flyers
* Concert discovery apps
* Ticket stubs
* Arena marquees

Not inspired by:

* Sports betting apps
* Corporate SaaS products
* Esports dashboards
* Casino interfaces

---

### Community Driven

The platform exists for fans.

The experience should feel welcoming, authentic, and fan-focused.

# Event Media Philosophy

## Poster Optional Design

Event posters are enhancements.

They are never required.

The platform must function beautifully without artwork.

---

## Event Priority Hierarchy

1. Event Name

2. Promotion

3. Date & Time

4. Venue

5. City & State

6. Event Status

7. Poster Artwork

"Does this help a fan discover a wrestling event faster?"

If the answer is yes, continue.

If the answer is no, simplify.
